THE FOUNDATION

A Legacy Carved in Time

Founded in 1984 by Arthur Sterling, Heirloom Joinery began as a small workshop dedicated to the near-forgotten art of traditional English cabinetry. Arthur believed that wood is a living history, and every piece of furniture should be built to witness at least three generations.

Today, while our tools have evolved with architectural precision, our hands-on philosophy remains unchanged. We source only the finest sustainable hardwoods, treating each plank with the reverence it deserves. Every joint is measured twice and cut once, ensuring structural longevity that defies modern disposable trends.
